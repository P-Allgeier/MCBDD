if Fred is healthy (95%) there's a 0.5% chance it gives a false positive
if Fred is sick (5%) there's a 0.5% chance it gives a false negative

True positives TP: 
0.995×0.05=0.04975
False positives FP: 
0.005×0.95=0.00475

Fred gets a positive, so we have conditional probability

TP/(TP + FP) = 0.913
